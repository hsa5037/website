Oktoberfest (5 gallon batch)

Fermentables
  • 3 lbs Munich - Light 10L - US
  • 7 lbs Vienna - US
  • 0.5 lbs Carapils - Dextrine Malt - US
  • 0.75 lbs Caramel/Crystal 60 - US
  • 0.5 lbs Carapils - Dextrine Malt - US
Hops
  • 1 oz. Hallertau at 60 mins
  • 1 oz. Tettnang at 30 mins
  • 1 oz. Saaz at 10 mins
Yeast and Additions
  • Wyeast Bohemian Lager 2124
  • 1 oz. Irish Moss at 10 mins
Method
  1. Mash at ~152°F for 60 minutes
  2. Batch sparge with ~170°F water
  3. Chill to pitching temperature and pitch Bohemian Lager Yeast
  4. Ferment at 58°F for 5 days, then let naturally rise to 65°F
  5. Cold crash for 2 days after about 2 weeks
  6. Keg and carbonate

Peach Milkshake IPA (5 gallon batch)

Fermentables
  • 6 lbs Wheat Malt, BEL
  • 6 lbs Pale Malt (2 Row) US
  • 2 lbs Flaked Oats
  • 1 lb Lactose
Hops
  • 1 oz. Mosaic at First Wort Addition
  • 1 oz. Citra at 20 mins
  • 1 oz. Mosaic at 10 mins
  • 1 oz. Citra at 0 mins
  • 1 oz. Mosaic at 0 mins
  • 2 oz. Citra (4 Day Dry Hop)
  • 2 oz. Mosaic (4 Day Dry Hop)
Yeast and Additions
  • Wyeast 1056 American Ale
Method
  1. Mash at ~151°F for 75 minutes
  2. Batch sparge with ~170°F water
  3. Chill to pitching temperature and pitch American Ale Yeast
  4. Ferment at 65°F for 10 days
  5. Dry hop for 4 days
  6. Cold crash for 2 days
  7. Keg and carbonate
