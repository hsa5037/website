There's Something About Hummy

Born to Anne Maitland (now Anne McEntre) and Sohail Ahmed, I grew up in Easton, Pennsylvania. After graduating from Easton Area High School, I began attending Pennsylvania State University where I majored in Economics.

At Penn State, I was very active in the Penn State Dance Marathon (THON) and lead my fraternity efforts to raise over $100,000 to combat pediatric cancer. One of the biggest honors of my life was being selected by the organization to represent them in THON by dancing for 46 hours to raise awareness.

After graduating from Penn State, I shortly began working as an Information Technical Specialist at my alma mater, Easton Area School District. It is here that I was thrust into the world of tech and began to take classes to further my education in the field at Oregon State University. I am currently enrolled in the Computer Science program at OSU and hope to one day transition my career into Software Engineering.
